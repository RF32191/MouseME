# MouseMe Support

Need help with MouseMe? This page covers setup, common issues, and how to reach us.

---

## Getting Started

MouseMe works as a **phone controller** and **Mac receiver**. You need both on the same Wi‑Fi network.

1. **On your Mac** — Open MouseMe and leave **Receiving** turned on.
2. **On your Mac** — When prompted, enable **Accessibility** in System Settings → Privacy & Security → Accessibility → turn on **MouseMe**.
3. **On your iPhone or iPad** — Open MouseMe → **Connect** tab → tap **Allow** for Local Network access.
4. **On your iPhone or iPad** — Under **Computers nearby**, tap your Mac.
5. **On your iPhone or iPad** — Open the **Mouse** tab and drag on the trackpad. Your Mac cursor should move.

For step-by-step instructions inside the app, tap the **?** button on the Mouse tab (Mac setup guide).

## Common Issues

### My Mac doesn’t appear under “Computers nearby”

- Make sure **both devices are on the same Wi‑Fi** (not guest or isolated networks).
- On iPhone: Settings → MouseMe → enable **Local Network**.
- On Mac: keep MouseMe open with **Receiving** on.
- Try **manual connect**: Connect tab → enter your Mac’s IP and port **8237** (shown in the Mac app window).
- Restart Wi‑Fi on both devices if needed.

### The Mac cursor doesn’t move

- On Mac: confirm **Accessibility** is enabled for MouseMe in System Settings.
- On Mac: the receiver should show **Listening on port 8237**.
- On iPhone: Connect tab should show **Connected** to your Mac.
- Disconnect and reconnect from the Connect tab.

### Desk Slide or Air Mouse feels wrong

- Open **Settings** in MouseMe and adjust sensitivity or invert axes.
- For Desk Slide: lay the phone **face up**, portrait orientation, on a flat surface.
- For Air Mouse: hold the trigger and move the phone smoothly in the air.

### TV remote doesn’t work

- TV remote sends commands **through your Mac** to a Roku on the same network.
- Connect to your Mac first, then use **TV** tab → **Find TVs** or enter the Roku IP manually.
- A Roku device is required; other TV brands may not be supported.

### Bluetooth pairing

- Bluetooth is optional. Use it from Connect → **More ways** when Wi‑Fi discovery is unavailable.
- Keep both devices close and ensure Bluetooth is on.

## System Requirements

- iPhone or iPad with iOS/iPadOS 26.2 or later
- Mac with macOS 26.2 or later
- Same local Wi‑Fi network (recommended)

---

## Privacy

MouseMe does not require an account. Control data stays on your local network. See our [Privacy Policy](privacy-policy.md) for details.
